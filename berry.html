Fall Berry Blitz Tea

Just like The Beatles' song, this whimsical herbal tea will soothe & delight the soul. The blend of fruits & flowers is perfect for the whole family, and makes a great iced tea. You can add sweetener to further accent the fruit flavors or drink it sans sugar for a more crisp cup.

Serving Suggetion:

2 tbs Fall Berry Blitz Tea, 1 tbs grandberries (fresh or frozen), fresh mint, 6 oz boiled water, Honey, to taste (optional)
